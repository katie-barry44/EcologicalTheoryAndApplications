4 Week 3

In week 3 of the course, you will focus on how the environment alters community dynamics. This week of the course will be taught by Duygu Tolunay! Duygu is a PhD student working with Mariet Hefting in the Ecology and Biodiversity group who focuses on peat!

Just a reminder: this week is almost completely in person so please come to class in person!
